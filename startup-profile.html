📌 Pitch Summary

NovaMed AI is building the operating system for AI-augmented diagnostics in primary care. Our platform integrates directly with EHR systems to surface AI-generated diagnostic suggestions — reducing missed diagnoses by 40% and cutting time-to-diagnosis by 60%.

We're already deployed in 12 health systems across California with $280K ARR, growing at 22% month-over-month. Our seed round will fund expansion to 50 health systems and build out our FDA 510(k) clearance pathway.

🔍 Problem

15 million misdiagnoses occur annually in US primary care. Overburdened physicians spend <15 minutes per patient, missing critical diagnostic signals. The cost: $50B+ in avoidable downstream care.

💡 Solution

NovaMed's AI listens to patient-physician conversations in real time, cross-references against 10M+ medical records, and surfaces differential diagnoses ranked by probability and urgency — in under 3 seconds.
